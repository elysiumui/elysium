permissive code, protected brand.

## You may, without asking

- Use, modify, and redistribute the framework code for any purpose, including
  commercially, under the Apache-2.0 terms.
- State truthfully that your application is *"built with Elysium"* or
  *"powered by Elysium UI"*, including using the Marks in that referential
  sense (talks, articles, tutorials, package dependencies).
- Redistribute **unmodified** official releases with the Marks intact.

## You may not, without written permission

- Ship a **modified or forked distribution** of the framework or Designer under
  the "Elysium" name or with the Blue Morpho logo — pick your own name and
  logo for forks (the code license fully permits the fork itself).
- Use the Marks in a product name, company name, domain name, or logo in a way
  that suggests your product **is** Elysium or is endorsed by the Elysium
  project.
- Alter the logo or combine the Marks with your own branding.
